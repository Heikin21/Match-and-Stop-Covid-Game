Information

Common symptoms of COVID-19:

    Fever or Chills
    Cough or trouble breathing
    Muscle or body pain
    Loss of taste and smell
Other symptoms include:
fatigue,
headaches,
sore throats,
congestion or runny noses,
nausea or vomiting,
and diarrhea
